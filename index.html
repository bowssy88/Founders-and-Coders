So this is my first ever website. Let me try and explain what I am feeling right now. It's ecstaticness, and excitement, all in one. Poem: We Are All Hamsters 

We are all hamsters
Locked up in a cage,
Let me tell you why
It might fill your heart with rage

Age 21, we wake up early, and put on our best hamster suit
Some like a coffee
Before we all begin our route
Through the tunnels
Tunnels, so busy, so thin
As other hamsters panic and rush to get in

On time. We arrive,
And jump straight onto the hamster wheel
Of our desks where we run, and run,
Until out eyes get sore
The owners out there having fun
But demanding more and more
Run faster, run quicker
I promise, I’ll give you a treat
At the end of the month in your feeding dish you’ll have more to eat

At dinner. Drained, you're back at home
The burrow feels warm,
But you wish you were free to roam
In the wild, cage-less, as you please
Thoughts that stay with you until your body starts to seize
up. Retirement, now you’re sixty-five,
oh what a time, to be alive
the hamster suit comes off permanently.

So if your stuck in a job, or situation that you hate,
A toxic relationship or stress,
Too much on your plate
Just remember that enjoying your time is the key
For life is short and all humans must be free
Have courage, smile, take risks and prosper,
Fail just as much as Tottenham Hotspur

Take action, have confidence, it will all be OK,
Because we are not furry hamsters at the end of the day.
